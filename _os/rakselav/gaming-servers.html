Gaming Servers

Servers That Up Your Game

Fit to handle even the most populated, resource-intensive multiplayer titles, Rakselav.com’s servers help you take your game to a higher level.

To accommodate the largest online communities, you need a hosting solution that’s fit to handle the network load. We provide powerful, up-to-date servers dedicated exclusively to hosting each and every one of your online matches. Whether players are battling alongside four or one hundred other people, they will be assured of low latency, consistent performance, and high-quality network connections.

Always Online

Running your games on independent, dedicated servers means that multiplayer lobbies are always available to players, even when you aren’t.

Improved Responsiveness

Our servers minimise ping for each of your players, ensuring seamless, smooth performance even during moments of intense network load.

Appropriately Specced

We offer a variety of modern CPU and RAM configurations to handle the heavy requirements of even the most strenuous online titles.

Complete Control

Enjoy the administrative freedom to alter rulesets and implement modifications on supported titles, freshening up the gameplay experience.

Routine Support

In the event that you begin to experience online connectivity interruptions, our tireless support team is always on call to quickly resolve the issue.

Remote Management

Each of our configurations comes with feature-complete, intuitive control panels that allow you to manage, update, and administer your server.

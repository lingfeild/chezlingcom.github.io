Garry's Mod

Sales Price € 17.00

Pushing the limits of the sandbox genre, Garry’s Mod was developed with one goal in mind—to provide players with all of the tools and mechanics necessary to create their own, self-contained experiences. Itself initially created as a mod to the incredibly popular Half-Life 2, Garry’s Mod quickly became a cult phenomenon among creative-minded gamers.

Wielding the extremely versatile Physics and Tool Guns, players can choose to spawn objects, items, weapons, and even characters into their world, and manipulate them in order to create models, racetracks or even complete multiplayer games! Drawing from other titles running on Valve Corporation’s proprietary Source Engine—as well as from the variety of mods created by die-hard fans—Garry’s Mod gives players the freedom to be in complete control over their world.

Whether they’re recreating moments from their favourite games, exploring independent game development or building highly detailed environments to explore alongside their friends, almost every gamer chooses to do so in private Garry’s Mod servers.

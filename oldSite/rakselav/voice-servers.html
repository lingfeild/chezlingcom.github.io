MUMBLE

One of the premiere VoIP applications, Mumble is a free, open-source piece of software designed primarily to keep gamers connected to one another while playing online. With a focus on low-latency communication, even individuals in large, heavily populated servers can chat, discuss, and respond to one another without interruption. Moreover, the service automatically normalises the volume levels of every member of the server, eliminating the need for users to individually micromanage the equalisation levels of the chat.

Key Features:

  • Specialised plugins that support 3D positional audio in compatible games
  • Certificate-based authentication encryption, increasing chat privacy
  • Light resource consumption, improving client responsiveness
  • Automatic echo cancellation that dramatically enhances call clarity
  • Custom APIs that enable the use of contextual in-game overlays
  • Individual or root-level permissions settings

TEAMSPEAK 3

An established player in the VoIP space, Teamspeak 3 is well-regarded as one of the most flexible online chat channels. Active since its initial release in 2006, Teamspeak 3 continues to be widely used and appreciated due to the fine-tuned control that it provides to users with its five-tiered permissions hierarchy. Continuously updated by its development team, the service minimises network impact on individual users by downloading and applying file updates on an as-needed basis, ensuring that players spend less time waiting in lengthy, recurring download queues.

Key Features:

  • Integrated text chat functions, facilitating communication
  • Customisable client to suit the preferences of individual users or groups
  • Compatible across all operating systems and devices
  • Accessible SDKs, enabling third-party development and refinements
  • Improved collaborative capabilities thanks to file transfer support
  • Background noise reduction that helps balance audio levels
  • Offline functionality that enables communication across local area networks

DISCORD

An all-in-one chat service that blends voice and text chat into one intuitive interface, Discord is undoubtedly one of the most popular gaming VoIP platforms available today. Fully functional across mobile devices and operating systems, the service also offers the flexibility of choosing between a browser or client-based interface. With a large community consistently supporting the service with third-party plugins, Discord is routinely referred to as the most feature-complete and future-proof VoIP service.

Key Features:

  • Improved functionality thanks to in-game overlays and hotkeys
  • Encrypted data and information that ensures security and privacy
  • Supported integration with a number of popular video streaming services
  • Expanded capabilities due to automated administrative bots
  • All-in-one voice chat, text messaging, and file sharing integration
  • Increased administrative control over your server and other users
  • Automated moderation tools that help keep large channels running smoothly

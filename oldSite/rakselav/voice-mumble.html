Mumble

MUMBLE

One of the premiere VoIP applications, Mumble is a free, open-source piece of software designed primarily to keep gamers connected to one another while playing online. With a focus on low-latency communication, even individuals in large, heavily populated servers can chat, discuss, and respond to one another without interruption. Moreover, the service automatically normalises the volume levels of every member of the server, eliminating the need for users to individually micromanage the equalisation levels of the chat.

Key Features:

  • Specialised plugins that support 3D positional audio in compatible games
  • Certificate-based authentication encryption, increasing chat privacy
  • Light resource consumption, improving client responsiveness
  • Automatic echo cancellation that dramatically enhances call clarity
  • Custom APIs that enable the use of contextual in-game overlays
  • Individual or root-level permissions settings

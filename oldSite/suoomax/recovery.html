What is disaster recovery?

When it comes to protecting your IT assets, including information, software, and hardware, it’s essential that you have a plan in place to secure your information.

Disaster recovery is part of a well-rounded IT security plan, using predetermined procedures to help you protect your information in case of an emergency or unexpected IT event. These events could range in severity, and could include a natural disaster, electricity failure, or cyberattack.

Best-in-class disaster recovery services from Suoomax help businesses quickly restore affected IT software, hardware, and information, designing and implementing the necessary strategies to protect and recover data.

Our dedicated IT disaster recovery professionals strive to help our clients restore and recover their IT services promptly, providing quality benefits such as:

Specialised management

At Suoomax, our team of disaster recovery experts take charge of the design, implementation, planning, and execution of the recovery on an as-needed basis. This lets you focus on your day-to-day tasks while we take care of your data restoration.

Continuous testing

In addition to implementing your disaster recovery plan during a negative event – whether it be a malicious event or not – the disaster recovery professionals at Suoomax practice continuous backups and testing of the disaster recovery plan to ensure events run smoothly when your IT system is faced with a potential threat.

Desired recovery times

Our disaster recovery team is committed to meeting the recovery times laid out in your disaster recovery plan, acting upon the first signs of a negative IT event to implement your disaster recovery plan, while backing up your information on a regular basis.

Why should you protect your information?

To operate effectively, a business requires a complex system of hardware, software, and computing networks to collect, analyse, and store both their customer and internal information. Information protection and the ability to recover it in the midst of a disaster is essential throughout a variety of businesses, but the type of business often indicates how complex an IT system’s disaster recovery will be.

In the modern day, even the most secure information stored digitally is at risk of being hacked and used illicitly. Whether you choose to protect and keep your information in the cloud, on various types of software, or in a centralised data centre, it’s vital that your IT professionals are equipped with the knowledge and expertise needed to efficiently recover any lost information, or, at the very least, know how to uncover what information has been hacked.

By putting high-value, high-quality disaster recovery measures in place, both your clients and your IT professionals can rest assured that steps are in place to prevent data loss, manage potential losses or emergency situations, and discover information loss or hacking as it occurs.

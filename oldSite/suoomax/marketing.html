Modern Marketing for Modern Consumers

As the ways in which we interact with others online has changed, so have the ways in which consumers interact with brands. Now, more than ever, online buyers are orientated to making purchases by discovering feedback from other customers, along with the messaging of your brand.

No longer are consumers making purchases based solely on how a product or service is pitched to them; instead, the modern buyer is looking for:

Influential Approval

Gone are the days of a witty one-liner winning over potential customers. With access to various social media platforms, consumers are looking for product endorsements and reviews from influential online personalities or brands.

Social Media Presence

Social media platforms are key spaces for business and individual use alike. For companies, these platforms are the areas in which you showcase your products, services, and expertise in a creative way. For consumers, social media platforms allow them to learn more about your brand, who your products and services are for, and what others who have tried your products have to say.

Strong Brand Positioning

Your business’s story matters, and the way you tell your story online is through your brand positioning. Brand positioning is the way in which you’re referred to in a potential customer’s mind, and the right marketing efforts can help you stand out amongst the competition.

Expert Guidance

The right strategic marketing methods allow you to become an influential business in your industry through expert guidance and up-to-date content. By sharing valuable information with your audience through engaging email marketing or tutorials from influential online personalities, you can situate your business as an industry leader that consumers can count on.

Our Robust Range of Marketing Services

When it comes to digital marketing, our range of capabilities is unparalleled. We offer our clients superior marketing services, including:

Email Marketing

A key asset in a well-rounded digital marketing strategy is email marketing. Streamlined, concise, and engaging email marketing services from Suoomax are designed to cultivate genuine interactions with your subscribers via charming and high-value email content.

A sophisticated email marketing strategy from Suoomax consists of a multifaceted process which acts to:

  • Reach your subscribers through an online platform in which they spend a lot of time: their inbox.

  • Inform your subscribersof modern innovations or urgent information.

  • Cultivate your brand through a demonstration of your voice, tone, and aesthetic.

  • Drive customer action with click-through opportunities and enticing offers.

  • Measure your reach and test what types of content, offers, and information are most valuable to your buyers and your bottom line.

Interactive Chatbots

When a potential customer is browsing your website or social media page seeking information, a well-designed interactive chatbot enables them to get the information they need about your product or service instantly.

An interactive chatbot is simply a computer software capable of holding a valuable discussion with consumers in an accessible and informal fashion through aspects of artificial intelligence. Think of these interactive chatbots as an extension of your intellect, able to inform customers at a key point in their decision-making journey.

At Suoomax, we design interactive chatbots with the intention of driving action on your site and increasing your bottom line.

Influencer Marketing

On all social media platforms, visual and written content are used to cultivate engagement with brands, and a powerful aspect of social media marketing is influencer marketing.

Influencer marketing uses the voice and reach of popular or influential online personalities to place products and services in front of their followers and digital community. By investing in influencer marketing strategies, you can expand your brand recognition and pursue increased overall reach.

Branding

Your branding is your power in the online realm. Consider some of your most-loved products and services. Chances are, they have a powerful and recognisable brand both on and offline. The core assets of a well-rounded brand are:

  • Tagline and slogans

  • A recognisable logo

  • The public brand name and domain

  • The type of font (typography) used

  • Voice and tone of written content

  • Permanent or long-term influencers associated with a brand

At Suoomax, we create top-notch brands using the story of your business to connect with potential customers while building your potency as a go-to product or service. Strong branding allows you to tell a story using a range of core business assets, including:

  • Consistent messaging, both in your aesthetics and written content.

  • Your business ideals, mission, and values.

  • Representation of your visual brand identity in all marketing assets, such as your logo and chosen colour palate.

Social Media Marketing

Chances are, when you think of professional digital marketing services, social media marketing is the first aspect of innovative digital marketing. Social media marketing is the way in which your business shares its story, products, or services on popular platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. Additionally, social media marketing creates space for meaningful interactions and connections with your potential clients, sparking recognition with each post, like, or retweet.

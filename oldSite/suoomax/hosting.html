Cost-Effective Unmanaged Servers

Propel your business forward with supportive IT infrastructure solutions from Suoomax.

What are unmanaged servers?

Unmanaged servers allow for IT freedom for advanced teams with the expertise and capabilities needed to successfully manage their servers, software, and website. Not only do unmanaged servers allow for more freedom and customisation of services, but they are often more cost-effective, as the level and number of available services differs from managed servers.

With unmanaged servers, businesses are responsible for maintenance and protection, meaning that your company can take charge of your security and manage servers independently of the server provider.

Our unmanaged server solutions

We partner with sophisticated businesses to create the ideal server structure for their unique needs. If your business employees an experienced IT development team, unmanaged server services create space for increased performance and enhanced customisation.

Unmanaged server services from Suoomax boast benefits such as:

  • Cost-effectiveness. Due to the lack of dedicated support, unmanaged servers offer clients more budget-friendly solutions.

  • The opportunity for enhanced customisation. Businesses can get creative with unmanaged servers, as the development team is responsible for the installation and maintenance of software and programs used.

  • Increased capabilities for experienced IT teams. The ability to customise unmanaged servers in turn provides the opportunity for increased capabilities, with unique software combinations allowing your digital and online processes to be expanded upon.
